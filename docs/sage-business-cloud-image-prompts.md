# Sage Business Cloud — image prompts

**Read `docs/photography-direction.md` first.** It holds the house style —
bright warm daylight, vivid grade, genuine energy, mixed African and South
Asian professionals, and the confident-versus-cheesy line. Only the shot list
is below.

Three images for `/erp/sage-business-cloud`.

> **These three are currently in the old muted, faceless style and are due for
> replacement.** Save new files over the old ones with the same names — no
> code change needed.

## What this product is

Cloud accounting for **growing SMEs** that have outgrown spreadsheets but do
not need a full ERP. The register is a business on the way up — a distributor,
wholesaler or light manufacturer that has got its act together and is
expanding.

**One rule with extra force here:** no tax-authority branding of any kind. No
KRA, no SARS, no logos, crests, stamps or official letterheads. Generated
documents imitating a revenue authority are a genuine problem, not a stylistic
one.

## 1 — Reporting

**Ratio:** 4:3 · **File:** `sage-reporting.jpg`

> Bright, warm corporate photograph, 50mm lens, f/2.5, strong morning
> sunlight through a large window. A business owner at a desk in a modern,
> well-kept Nairobi office, looking up from a printed report with a pleased,
> confident expression — the month has gone well. A Black African woman in her
> forties in a smart bright blouse, ordinary professional build, face visible,
> not looking at camera. On the desk: an open laptop angled away with a dim
> screen, a mug, a neat stack of folders, a plant. Bright modern room, wall
> calendar, greenery through the window. Warm vivid grade, rich skin tones,
> clean whites. Negative space upper right.
>
> Avoid: readable screen content, spreadsheets or dashboards legible on
> screen, legible figures on paper, grinning at camera, thumbs up, dark or
> grey light, desaturated grade, cramped or dated offices, corporate
> boardrooms, suits and ties, logos or branding, any tax-authority marks,
> over-smoothed skin, extra fingers.

**Why:** the reports are for one person making a decision. Show that person,
and show the decision going well.

## 2 — Inventory

**Ratio:** 16:9 (wide band across the section) · **File:** `sage-inventory.jpg`

⚠ **A wide band, not a portrait slot.** Ask for **16:9** and keep the subject
central with room either side — the composition must survive a crop.

> Bright, warm corporate photograph, 35mm lens, f/2.8, generous daylight
> flooding in through a wide roller door. Inside a well-organised wholesale
> storeroom in the Nairobi industrial area: tidy metal racking stacked with
> cartons and sacks, clean concrete floor. Two colleagues mid-aisle, one
> checking a tablet against the shelves and the other pointing something out,
> both engaged and one smiling — stock is under control and business is busy.
> A Black African man in his thirties and a South Asian woman in her forties,
> ordinary professional builds, smart work shirts. Faces visible, neither
> looking at camera. Bright warm light from the doorway, rich colour, deep
> clean shadows. Wide composition with space either side.
>
> Avoid: branded packaging or product logos, readable labels, readable
> screens, grinning at camera, thumbs up, high-vis fashion shoots, forklifts
> in motion, dark or grey light, desaturated grade, empty or derelict
> warehouses, poverty framing, logos or branding, over-smoothed skin, extra
> fingers.

**Why:** inventory is the module that decides whether the software fits. A
real, busy, well-run storeroom says "we understand your business".

## 3 — Records and VAT

**Ratio:** 4:3 · **File:** `sage-records.jpg`

> Bright, warm corporate photograph, 50mm lens, f/2.5, abundant daylight.
> Two colleagues at a light desk in a modern Nairobi office, working through
> an open lever-arch file of neatly filed receipts, one holding a page and
> the other making a note — organised, on top of it, one smiling. A Black
> African man in his forties and a Black African woman in her thirties,
> ordinary professional builds, smart shirts with colour. Faces visible,
> neither looking at camera. An open laptop just behind, screen dim. The
> papers show ruled columns and structure but no legible words, and carry no
> letterhead, stamp, crest or logo of any kind. Warm vivid grade, clean bright
> whites, shallow depth of field.
>
> Avoid: legible text or figures on any document, letterheads, official
> stamps, crests or seals, any revenue-authority branding (KRA, SARS or
> otherwise), currency notes, calculators as hero props, messy-desk clichés,
> readable screens, grinning at camera, dark or grey light, desaturated grade,
> logos or branding, over-smoothed skin, extra fingers.

**Why:** "audit-ready" is an abstraction. Two people on top of their records,
in good light, is what it looks like.
