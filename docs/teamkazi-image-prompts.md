# TeamKazi — image prompts

**Read `docs/photography-direction.md` first.** It holds the house style —
bright warm daylight, vivid grade, genuine energy, mixed African and South
Asian professionals, and the confident-versus-cheesy line. Only the shot list
is below.

Four images for `/business-applications/teamkazi`.

> **These four are currently in the old muted, faceless style and are due for
> replacement.** Save new files over the old ones with the same names — no
> code change needed.

## What this product is

Project management for **professional services firms** that bill for their
people's time — architects, engineers, consultants, surveyors. The register is
a practice that is winning work and making money on it. Rolled drawings,
printed schedules and site folders do more here than laptops do.

**One caution specific to this page.** TeamKazi's whole argument is that it
does not flatter you. The imagery should read as *confident and in control*,
not *triumphant* — a firm that knows its numbers, rather than one celebrating.
Warm and energetic, but grounded.

## 1 — Know your margin

**Ratio:** 4:3 · **File:** `teamkazi-margin.jpg`

> Bright, warm corporate photograph, 50mm lens, f/2.5, strong morning
> sunlight through a large window. A principal of an engineering or
> consulting practice sits at a desk in a modern, well-appointed Nairobi
> office, looking up from a printed report with a satisfied, confident
> expression — the numbers are good. A South Asian man in his fifties in a
> crisp open-collar shirt, ordinary professional build, face visible, not
> looking at camera. On the desk: a rolled set of drawings, a scale rule, a
> marked-up printout, a coffee cup, an open laptop angled away with a dim
> screen. Behind, a bright office with plants and glass. Warm vivid grade,
> rich skin tones, clean whites. Negative space upper right.
>
> Avoid: grinning at camera, thumbs up, readable screen content, legible
> figures on paper, dark or grey light, desaturated grade, cramped or dated
> offices, suits and ties, logos or branding, over-smoothed skin, extra
> fingers.

## 2 — Let the AI do the setup

**Ratio:** 4:3 · **File:** `teamkazi-scope-document.jpg`

> Bright, warm corporate photograph, 50mm lens, f/2.5, abundant daylight. Two
> colleagues at a light timber desk in a modern Nairobi office, one flattening
> a thick printed scope document while the other points at a section, both
> engaged and one smiling — the plan is coming together quickly. A Black
> African woman in her thirties and a South Asian man in his forties,
> ordinary professional builds, smart shirts with colour. Faces visible,
> neither looking at camera. An open laptop just behind, screen dim and
> indistinct. The printed page shows paragraph structure but no legible words.
> Warm vivid grade, clean bright whites, shallow depth of field.
>
> Avoid: legible text on the document, readable screens, grinning at camera,
> handshakes, contracts with signatures, dark or grey light, desaturated
> grade, logos or branding, over-smoothed skin, extra fingers.

**Why:** the document is the input. This is the one image showing where the
plan comes from, so the AI claim lands as a workflow rather than a buzzword.

## 3 — Numbers you can take to a client meeting

**Ratio:** 4:3 · **File:** `teamkazi-client-meeting.jpg`

> Bright, warm corporate photograph, 50mm lens, f/2.5, generous daylight from
> a window. Three people around a light meeting table in a modern Nairobi
> office, a printed report open between them, one explaining a figure with an
> open confident gesture while the others follow — a number being asked about
> and answered well. A mix of Black African and South Asian men and women,
> mid-30s to mid-50s, ordinary professional builds, smart business dress with
> some colour. Faces visible, engaged, nobody looking at camera. Water
> glasses, notebooks, a closed laptop. Warm vivid grade, rich skin tones,
> clean whites.
>
> Avoid: grinning at camera, handshakes, thumbs up, applause, legible report
> content, readable screens, dark or grey light, desaturated grade, suits and
> ties, glass towers, projector screens, charts on walls, logos or branding,
> over-smoothed skin, extra fingers.

**Why:** the "no flattering defaults" argument only matters at this exact
moment — when someone else is looking at your numbers, and they hold up.

## 4 — If you bill for time

**Ratio:** 3:2 · **File:** `teamkazi-timesheets.jpg`

> Bright, warm corporate photograph, 35mm lens, f/2.8, golden late-afternoon
> sunlight raking across a desk. A project manager at a tidy desk in a modern
> Nairobi office, finishing the week — leaning back slightly from an open
> laptop with a relaxed, satisfied expression, a notebook of week's notes
> beside them. A Black African woman in her forties in a bright blouse,
> ordinary professional build, face visible, not looking at camera. Warm low
> sun through a window casting long soft shadows. The laptop screen is dim
> and angled away. Warm vivid grade, rich skin tones, amber light against
> clean surfaces. Generous negative space on the left.
>
> Avoid: readable screens, timesheet or spreadsheet UI, grinning at camera,
> clocks or hourglasses as symbols, dark or grey light, desaturated grade,
> empty tidy flat-lays, overhead knolling, logos or branding, over-smoothed
> skin, extra fingers.

**Why:** the weekly ritual TeamKazi replaces. Friday evening with the
timesheets already done — which is a genuinely good feeling to sell.
