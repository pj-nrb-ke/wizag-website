# WizCRM — image prompts

**Read `docs/photography-direction.md` first.** It holds the house style —
bright warm daylight, vivid grade, genuine energy, mixed African and South
Asian professionals, and the confident-versus-cheesy line. Only the shot list
is below.

Four images for `/business-applications/wizcrm`.

> **These four are currently in the old muted, faceless style and are due for
> replacement.** Save new files over the old ones with the same names — no
> code change needed.

## What this product is

An AI-first CRM for **field sales teams**. The people in these frames are reps
on the road and the managers who run them. The register is a team that is
selling well and knows it — mobile, energetic, out in the market.

## 1 — AI Visit Capture

**Ratio:** 4:3 · **File:** `wizcrm-visit-capture.jpg`

> Bright, warm corporate photograph, 35mm lens, f/2.8, strong late-morning
> Kenyan sunlight. A sales representative stands just outside a busy
> hardware and building-supplies shop in Nairobi, speaking into a smartphone
> held near their mouth, mid-sentence and clearly pleased — the visit has
> gone well. A Black African man in his forties, ordinary professional build,
> in a crisp short-sleeved shirt with a lanyard, a slim folder under one arm.
> Shot from three-quarters front so his face and expression are visible.
> Behind him the shopfront is stacked with paint tins and coiled hosepipe,
> lively and well-kept. Warm ochre and bright sunlight, rich skin tones,
> shallow depth of field. Energetic and optimistic, the middle of a good
> working day. Negative space on the left.
>
> Avoid: grinning at camera, thumbs up, glowing or readable phone screens,
> floating UI or holograms, dark or grey light, desaturated grade, poverty
> framing, safari or wildlife motifs, logos or branding, over-smoothed skin,
> extra fingers.

**Why:** the twenty-second habit actually happening, in the environment the
buyer recognises as their own patch — and visibly working.

## 2 — Wanjiru, the virtual sales manager

**Ratio:** 4:3 · **File:** `wizcrm-morning-brief.jpg`

> Bright, warm corporate photograph, 50mm lens, f/2.5, golden early-morning
> sunlight streaming through a window. A sales manager sits at a tidy desk in
> a modern Nairobi office, reading a laptop screen with a cup of tea beside
> her and a relaxed, satisfied expression — the day is already under control.
> A Black African woman in her forties in a bright blouse, ordinary
> professional build. Shot from three-quarters front, face visible. The room
> is light and well-appointed: a plant, a clean whiteboard, a window with a
> jacaranda outside. The laptop screen is dim and indistinct. Warm vivid
> grade, rich skin tones, clean whites. Calm and confident rather than
> hurried. Negative space upper right.
>
> Avoid: readable screen content, grinning at camera, dark or grey light,
> desaturated grade, cluttered or dated offices, headsets, multiple monitors,
> glowing interfaces, logos or branding, over-smoothed skin, extra fingers.

**Why:** Wanjiru replaces the morning status-chase. Show the calm it
produces — and that calm should look like success, not resignation.

## 3 — AI-assisted prospecting

**Ratio:** 4:3 · **File:** `wizcrm-prospecting.jpg`

> Bright, warm corporate photograph, 50mm lens, f/2.5, abundant daylight from
> a large window. Two colleagues stand together at a desk looking at a
> laptop, one leaning in and pointing at something on the screen with
> obvious enthusiasm, the other laughing in response — they have found a good
> lead. A South Asian woman in her thirties and a Black African man in his
> forties, ordinary professional builds, smart shirts with some colour, no
> jackets. Faces visible, neither looking at camera. A bright modern meeting
> space in Nairobi with plants and glass. The screen is angled away and
> indistinct. Warm vivid grade, rich skin tones, shallow depth of field.
>
> Avoid: readable screens, charts or dashboards legible on screen, grinning at
> camera, thumbs up, high fives, dark or grey light, desaturated grade, suits
> and ties, glass towers, glowing data visualisations, logos or branding,
> over-smoothed skin, extra fingers.

**Why:** prospecting is a judgement. Two people enjoying a good call on a
scored lead is the "explainable, no black box" claim made human.

## 4 — Built for the field

**Ratio:** 3:2 · **File:** `wizcrm-in-the-field.jpg`

> Bright, warm corporate photograph, 35mm lens, f/2.8, golden late-afternoon
> Kenyan sunlight. A sales representative stands beside a clean white pickup
> truck at the roadside, checking a phone between calls with a satisfied
> half-smile, a folder tucked under one arm. A Black African woman in her
> thirties in a smart short-sleeved shirt, ordinary professional build. Shot
> three-quarters front, face visible, not looking at camera. Behind: a tarmac
> road, tidy roadside shops with painted signage, an acacia in the middle
> distance, warm dust. Low golden light raking across the scene, long soft
> shadows. Warm vivid grade, rich tones against a bright sky. Wide
> composition with generous negative space on the right.
>
> Avoid: grinning at camera, glowing phone screens, staged posing, brand-new
> showroom vehicles, safari or wildlife, poverty or hardship framing, tourist
> imagery, sunset silhouettes, drone or aerial views, dark or desaturated
> grade, logos or branding, over-smoothed skin, extra fingers.

**Why:** the most differentiating image on the page. "Built for how you
actually sell" — and the rep should look like someone having a good week.
